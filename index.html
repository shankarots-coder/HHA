About Us

Kotdwara Sahodaya School Complex is a collaborative forum of 18 CBSE-affiliated schools of Kotdwara, formed in accordance with the norms and vision of the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The Sahodaya movement embodies the spirit of “togetherness for excellence”, bringing schools together on a common platform to share ideas, resources, and best practices in education. Kotdwara Sahodaya aims to foster professional growth among educators, encourage innovation in teaching–learning practices, and promote holistic development of students. By working collectively, member schools strive to enrich academic standards while nurturing values, life skills, and responsible citizenship among learners.

Our Vision

To create a vibrant, inclusive, and future-ready educational ecosystem where schools collaborate rather than compete, ensuring quality education that empowers students to become confident, compassionate, and competent global citizens rooted in Indian values.

Our Mission

To promote collaborative learning and professional development among school leaders and teachers To facilitate academic enrichment, training programmes, workshops, and student-centric activities To encourage innovation, creativity, and experiential learning in line with CBSE’s competency-based education framework To nurture ethical values, social responsibility, and national integration through joint initiatives To serve as a platform for sharing best practices, challenges, and solutions in school education

Our Commitment

Kotdwara Sahodaya School Complex is committed to strengthening the quality of education in the region by fostering unity, academic excellence, and continuous improvement. Together, we envision shaping schools that prepare learners not just for examinations, but for life.

KOTDWARA SAHODAYA SCHOOLS COMPLEX (KOTSSC)BYE LAWS

OBJECTIVES:

"Coming together is a beginning; thinking together is progress; working together is success". All the CBSE schools of Kotdwara zone have come together with the motive of "man making, character molding and nation building process". Kotdwara Sahodaya Schools Complex, henceforth, mentioned as KOTSSC will be a platform to bring out the latent potential of not only the students but also the entire teaching fraternity by involving all the stake holders of various member schools. KOTSSC will be a substratum where all its member schools can grow by helping each other symbiotically.

ACTIVITIES:

  • Conducting orientation programmes for the Heads and teaching fraternity through the agencies empanelled by the board.
  • Keeping all the schools informed about the circulars and other information given by the board.
  • Providing information regarding the procedure for getting affiliation from the board.
  • Organizing programs or competitions in Scholastic and Co- Scholastic areas to nurture the latent potential of students.
  • Conducting assessments with common question papers for the interested schools, especially for classes IX and X.
  • Providing details about the mentors as well as resource persons recognized by the board.
  • Voicing out the problems of the schools to the board.
  • Providing a platform to share the best practices of every school.

BYE LAWS:

Kotdwara Sahodaya Schools Complexes (KOTSSC) is a voluntary association of schools within Kotdwara Zone which have agreed to come together for a systematic and system-wide renewal of the total educational process. The major areas to begin with for collaboration among schools of the complex are:

  • Human resource mobilization.
  • Professional growth of teachers.
  • Value oriented school climate evaluation.
  • Evaluation.
  • Effective education management.
  • Promoting vocational education.

Kotdwara Sahodaya Schools Complex has come to existence in July, 2016. At present there are about 15 members in the complex.
